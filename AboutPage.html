Features


(1) Ensures hospitals have good supply or inventories of blood bags.

(2) List the availability of blood bags at any given time.

(3) Ability to manage the information of its blood donor.

(4) Alerts for blood requirement from registered donors.

(5) Auto-check if the person donated blood in the last 3 months.

(6) Allows good documentation about the donor and their blood donation activities.

(7) Support fast searching to find match blood bags for the right person.

(8) Effectively manage blood camps
